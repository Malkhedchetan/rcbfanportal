Welcome to the Official RCB Fan Portal


Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) is a founding team of the Indian Premier League (IPL), based in Bangalore, Karnataka, and owned by United Spirits Limited, a Diageo Group company. Known for its massive fan following, RCB has featured star players like Virat Kohli, AB de Villiers, and Chris Gayle Despite reaching the finals in 2009, 2011, and 2016, the team is yet to win an IPL title. RCB plays its home matches at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium and is famous for its vibrant red and black jersey, passionate fans, and initiatives like the "Go Green" campaign.
